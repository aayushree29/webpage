why eat food?

Eating a variety of plant-based foods from the 5 major
food groups provides a range of health benefits, such as lower blood pressure,
triglyceride levels, glucose and waist circumference.1 Additionally,
food is what fuels you and delivers the calories and nutrients your body
needs to function properly.

Eating a healthy, balanced diet is one of the most important
things you can do to protect your health. In fact, up to 80% of premature heart disease and stroke can be prevented through your life choices and habits, such as eating a healthy diet and being physically active.
